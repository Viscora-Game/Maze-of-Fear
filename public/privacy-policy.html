Privacy Policy for Maze of Fear

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Maze of Fear ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by the developer. The Application is a procedural maze exploration game.

1. What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information: The Application does not require any user registration. You do not need to provide any personal information to use the Application.

Automatically Collected Information: The Application does not automatically collect or transmit any personal data. It uses device-local storage (localStorage) solely to save your game progress (current level, custom HUD button layout, volume level, vibration and shadow settings) on your own device. This data is never sent to any server.

2. Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

No. This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

3. Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

No. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, no third parties have access to any information.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data, parents can be assured that their children's privacy is fully protected.

5. Security

We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information, but since the Application does not collect or store personal data, security breaches regarding personal information are not applicable.

6. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, please contact us via our developer channel.
